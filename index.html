About

Welcome to E-Week 2023!

E-Week is a week-long celebration highlighting the contributions to society that engineers make. Started in 1951 by the National Society of Professional Engineers, this particular week in February is observed by a plethora of professional and academic societies as well as corporations and government agencies. For students here at Illinois, there are community and competitive events, guest speakers, and food. Register an account for a 5 or 10 man team to participate in the festivities and competitions. Awards, prizes, and bragging rights await!

Below is an overview of all events, including a description and any relevant information like point tier, host, and location. All events have two competitions (5-Person and 10-Person) unless stated otherwise. Points & event winners will be updated every night. Rules are linked in the event titles. If you have any general questions, email Sofia Ramos at sramo2@illinois.edu.
